Types of Fraud We Help With

Romance Scams

Fake online relationships designed to steal your money through emotional manipulation

Investment Fraud

Fake crypto, forex, or business opportunities promising unrealistic returns

Phishing & ID Theft

Fake emails, texts, or websites stealing your passwords and personal information

Shopping Scams

Fake online stores, non-delivery of goods, or counterfeit products

Job Scams

Fake job offers requiring upfront payments or personal information

Charity Scams

Fake charities exploiting disasters or emergencies to steal donations
