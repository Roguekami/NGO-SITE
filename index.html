Why Donations Matter

Your contributions directly fund life-changing programs. We maintain complete financial transparency with quarterly reports showing exactly how donations are used. On average, 95% of all funds go directly to program services, with only 5% for essential administrative costs.

Every donation helps provide emergency housing, legal assistance for accessing government aid, essential supplies, trauma counseling, job training, and community rebuilding resources. Your generosity transforms lives.

Important Legal Disclaimer

Hope Rebuild is an independent nonprofit organization. We are not a government agency and do not process government payments, refunds, or disaster relief funds directly.

We provide guidance and support to help disaster survivors navigate government assistance programs, understand tax relief options, and complete applications. We connect people with official government resources and advocate on their behalf, but all official government aid comes from government agencies, not from our organization.

Recognition & Partnerships

We are a registered nonprofit organization with full government recognition and certification. We partner with national emergency management agencies, local government units, international relief organizations, and community groups to maximize our impact.

Our work has been recognized by humanitarian organizations and featured in media coverage highlighting effective disaster response programs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for disaster relief assistance?

Anyone who has been directly affected by a natural disaster may be eligible for our services. This includes those who have lost homes, sustained property damage, or experienced financial hardship due to disasters.

How long does it take to receive help?

Our emergency response team contacts applicants within 24 hours of submission. Emergency housing can typically be arranged within 48-72 hours depending on availability.

Do I have to pay for your services?

No. All our disaster relief services are provided free of charge to survivors. We are funded entirely by donations and grants.

Can you help me get government disaster relief money?

We provide guidance and assistance with completing government aid applications, but we cannot guarantee approval. All decisions about government assistance are made by the relevant government agencies.

Emergency Housing Program

Our flagship program provides immediate shelter solutions for families who have lost their homes to floods, fires, storms, and other natural disasters. We deploy movable housing units within 48-72 hours of application approval, ensuring families have safe, dignified temporary accommodation.

What We Provide:

✓ Movable Home Units: Fully equipped temporary housing with beds, kitchen, and bathroom
✓ Essential Furniture: Tables, chairs, storage, and sleeping arrangements
✓ Basic Utilities: Connection to water, electricity where available
✓ Household Supplies: Cooking utensils, bedding, hygiene products
✓ Food Packages: Initial 2-week supply of non-perishables
✓ Ongoing Support: Regular check-ins and maintenance

How Your Donation Helps:

₦50,000 sponsors one complete movable home unit for a family (6 months)
₦15,000 funds one month of housing support including utilities and maintenance
₦5,000 provides essential furniture and household items for one family
₦2,000 supplies a 2-week emergency food package

Program Success Story

"After the floods destroyed our home, we had nowhere to go. Hope Rebuild provided us with a movable home within 3 days. It gave our family shelter, safety, and hope. We lived there for 8 months while rebuilding our permanent home. We are forever grateful."
— Mrs. Adebayo, Lagos State

Government Aid Navigation Program

Navigating government assistance can be overwhelming during a crisis. Our expert team guides disaster survivors through complex application processes, helps gather required documentation, and advocates on your behalf to access available government relief programs, tax benefits, and recovery grants.

Services We Provide:

✓ Application Assistance: Help completing NEMA and state emergency forms
✓ Document Preparation: Organizing proof of loss and identity documents
✓ Legal Support: Pro bono legal aid for complex cases
✓ Tax Relief Guidance: Understanding disaster-related tax deductions
✓ Follow-up Services: Tracking application status and appeals
✓ Advocacy: Representing you in government communications

How Your Donation Helps:

₦25,000 funds complete application support for 5 families
₦10,000 provides legal assistance and representation for one family's claims
₦3,000 covers document preparation, filing fees, and photocopies
₦1,500 supports follow-up services and status tracking for one case

Program Success Story

"I didn't know where to start with government aid applications. The forms were confusing and I kept getting rejected. Hope Rebuild helped me organize my documents, fill out the forms correctly, and even spoke to officials on my behalf. I received ₦180,000 in disaster relief that I would have never gotten alone."
— Mr. Okonkwo, Anambra State

Community Rebuilding Initiative

Disaster recovery doesn't end with emergency relief. Our Community Rebuilding Initiative focuses on long-term sustainable recovery through job training, mental health support, infrastructure restoration, and economic empowerment. We work alongside community leaders to create resilient neighborhoods that are better prepared for future challenges.

Our Comprehensive Approach:

✓ Job Training Programs: Skills development for displaced workers
✓ Mental Health Support: Trauma counseling and support groups
✓ Small Business Grants: Helping entrepreneurs restart operations
✓ Infrastructure Repair: Rebuilding community centers and schools
✓ Youth Programs: Educational support for affected children
✓ Disaster Preparedness: Training communities for future resilience

How Your Donation Helps:

₦35,000 funds rebuilding materials for community spaces (market stalls, meeting halls)
₦20,000 supports vocational training programs for 10 people (3-month courses)
₦8,000 provides trauma counseling sessions for one affected family (8 sessions)
₦5,000 offers small business restart grants or equipment

Program Success Story

"Our entire market was destroyed in the fire. Hope Rebuild didn't just help us rebuild the stalls—they trained 25 of us in new skills like tailoring and phone repair. They also provided counseling because many of us were traumatized. Today our market is thriving again and we're stronger than before."
— Chief Nnamdi, Community Leader, Imo State

About Hope Rebuild

A trusted nonprofit dedicated to helping communities recover from natural disasters

Our Story

Hope Rebuild was founded in 2020 in response to the growing need for coordinated disaster relief services in Nigeria. After witnessing the devastating impact of floods, fires, and storms on vulnerable communities, our founders brought together experts in emergency response, government relations, and community development to create a comprehensive support system for disaster survivors.

Today, we serve thousands of families annually across Nigeria, providing not just immediate emergency relief but long-term recovery support that helps communities rebuild stronger and more resilient than before.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, effective disaster relief services that help families recover with dignity and rebuild stronger, more resilient communities. We believe every person deserves support during their darkest hours and the resources needed to restore their lives.

Our Values

Compassion First

We treat every person with dignity, empathy, and respect
