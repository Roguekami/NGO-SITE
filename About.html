About NGO World Foundation

Committed to restoring hope and rebuilding lives in disaster-affected communities across the globe since 2015

Our Story

Born from tragedy, driven by compassion, dedicated to making a lasting difference

The NGO World Foundation was established in 2015 in the aftermath of devastating floods that displaced thousands of families in Southeast Asia. Our founder, Dr. Amina Hassan, witnessed firsthand the chaos, confusion, and desperation that follows natural disasters‚Äînot just from the disaster itself, but from the complex bureaucracy survivors faced when trying to access help.

What started as a small volunteer effort providing temporary shelter to 50 families has grown into an internationally recognized disaster relief organization. Today, we operate in 12 countries across Africa, Asia, and the Caribbean, serving thousands of disaster survivors annually.

Our approach is holistic: we don't just provide immediate relief‚Äîwe walk alongside communities through their entire recovery journey, from emergency response to long-term rebuilding and resilience planning.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our mission to serve disaster-affected communities

2015

Foundation Established

NGO World Foundation founded in Lagos, Nigeria, providing emergency shelter to 50 families affected by flooding.

2017

Government Aid Program Launch

Introduced specialized services to help survivors navigate complex government assistance applications.

2019

Regional Expansion

Extended operations to Haiti, Philippines, and India, serving over 1,000 families annually.

2021

Community Rebuilding Initiative

Launched comprehensive long-term recovery programs focusing on economic empowerment and resilience.

2024

Global Recognition

Received international humanitarian awards and reached milestone of 2,500 families served.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision and action we take

Compassion First

Every person we serve is treated with dignity, respect, and genuine care. We never forget that behind every case is a family in crisis.

Transparency

We maintain complete financial transparency with regular public reporting. Donors and beneficiaries always know where resources go.

Speed & Efficiency

In disaster relief, time matters. We prioritize rapid response while maintaining quality and accountability in all our work.

Community-Centered

We don't impose solutions‚Äîwe listen to communities and empower them to lead their own recovery journey.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of professional service delivery and continuous improvement.

Sustainability

Our programs build long-term resilience, not dependency. We create lasting positive change that extends beyond our direct intervention.
