Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for disaster relief assistance?

Anyone who has been directly affected by a natural disaster may be eligible for our services. This includes those who have lost homes, sustained property damage, or experienced financial hardship due to disasters.

How long does it take to receive help?

Our emergency response team contacts applicants within 24 hours of submission. Emergency housing can typically be arranged within 48-72 hours depending on availability.

Do I have to pay for your services?

No. All our disaster relief services are provided free of charge to survivors. We are funded entirely by donations and grants.

Can you help me get government disaster relief money?

We provide guidance and assistance with completing government aid applications, but we cannot guarantee approval. All decisions about government assistance are made by the relevant government agencies.
